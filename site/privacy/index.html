Pime Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

1. Personal Information We Collect

Pime does not operate its own servers or collect users’ photos or app settings.

Photo capture, saving, tagging, and settings are processed only on your device. For information that Google may process while serving ads, see “Advertising Services” below.

2. Permissions

Pime uses only the following permissions for its core features.

  • Camera: Used to take photos with a timestamp watermark.
  • Photo Library: Used to save captured photos to your device and import photos from your gallery.
  • Location: Used to show the current address in the watermark when you enable location display.

Photos and location information accessed through these permissions are not provided to advertising services. When converting a location into an address, your device operating system’s location and reverse-geocoding services may process location information. Pime does not store this information on its own servers.

3. Advertising Services

Pime uses Google AdMob to support app operations. Ad requests are limited to non-personalized ads (NPA).

Google may process IP addresses, device information, ad interaction information, and diagnostic information for ad delivery, security, fraud prevention, and performance measurement. For details, see the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

Photos captured in the app and app settings are stored only on your device. Deleting the app removes all such data from the device.

5. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact hagi4u@gmail.com.
